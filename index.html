    Projects overview

    A customer segment is group of customers sharing needs and preferences.
    Segmenting precisely helps building a focused offering.
    Segmentation is usually based on demographics (location, age, gender, number of children ...) or business type (size, sector, growth rate...).

    Product features are what makes your offer unique.
    They are complementary if they contribute to satisfy the same customer need.

    Resources are the materials, capabilities, tools and skills which are essential to your business. Identifying them is key to maximize their use.

    Monetization is how you extract value from your offer: fees, subscriptions, advertizing...
    It impacts how customers buy (how often, how much...) and how recurring and predictable your revenues are.

    Price is not only the most tangible way for your customers to compare you to competition, it's also part of your brand image.
    Pricing techniques (bundle offers, rebates...) can be used to sell more, eliminate stocks...

    Distribution refers to the channels through which you sell and deliver to customers.

    Communication refers to the channels through which you convey messages and get feedback from customers.

    Production starts with design and ends with building. The shorter the production cycle, the faster the feedback.

    Competitors answers the same customer need as you. Let's see how your different!
